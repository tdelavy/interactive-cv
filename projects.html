Entrepreneurship Project - AI-Powered Lost & Found Services

As part of an entrepreneurship course, I developed and presented an AI-powered Apple app aimed at transforming the lost-and-found process. The application allows users to upload images of their items. By leveraging generative AI, the system generates precise descriptions of the objects based on the input provided. These descriptions are then compared against newly reported found items using advanced matching algorithms, considering both attributes and location data. Once a match is identified, the app connects the owner with the finder.

MindMap AI: Simplifying Research Exploration

MindMap AI is a tool designed to streamline the process of exploring and answering complex questions using your saved research papers. By leveraging advanced technologies like GROBID and sentence transformers, it analyzes abstracts to identify the most relevant papers for your queries. The system generates a dynamic mindmap where key papers are visually represented for easy access. With interactive features, you can view abstracts, ask questions, and synthesize insights from multiple sources efficiently.
